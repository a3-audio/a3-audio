Interact live with 3D Audio

Vision

3D audio is the future of music production and listening.

We design easy-to-use hardware interfaces to control 3D sound, live or in the studio. Our system lowers the entrance barrier for the performing artist and lets you get creative with spatial audio effects.

Our mission is to spread 3D audio to clubs and venues. We plan and deploy custom audio solutions for event organizers to deliver a new quality of immersive experience to your audiences.

We involve our community in the open development process and provide a platform for technical and artistic exchange. Free licensing of software and hardware makes our solution extensible to blend into your individual workflow.

System

A³ Motion

4-Channel Motion Sampler

  • control motion via touch
  • 4 patterns per channel
  • beat-synced
  • OSC output

A³ Mixer

4-Channel DJ Mixer

  • control spatialization per channel
  • pre-listen binaural 3D
  • FX section
  • OSC output

A³ Core

3D Sound Server

  • spatialization, mixing, effects
  • speaker management
  • customizable audio hardware
  • full control via OSC

About us

We are a team of spatial audio enthusiasts located in Berlin and Aachen.

Coming from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, we combine our knowledge in interaction design, music technology, computer science and electrical engineering. We share several years of experience in planning and operating public address 3D sound systems.

Together, we want to realize our vision to bring 3D audio to the masses.
